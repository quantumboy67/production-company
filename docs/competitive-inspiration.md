# Competitive Inspiration

This document captures product patterns worth studying without copying another company's brand, interface, or operating model. Juniper Berry Productions should stay focused on live-event production planning and financial reconciliation.

## FoxOMS / Sonderplan Patterns Worth Emulating

FoxOMS and Sonderplan point toward useful operating patterns for future Juniper Berry planning work:

- Resource-aware calendar
- Rooms/venues, people, equipment, and bookings visible in one operating view
- Collapsible grouped schedules to reduce noise
- Booking/resource conflict detection
- Right-side slide-out editor for calendar/event edits
- Event-specific custom statuses and structured metadata
- Missing invoice/receipt visibility
- Estimated/projected vs actual/paid reporting
- Export options: CSV, PDF, print, calendar feed
- Future integration layer/API mindset

## Boundaries

- Do not copy FoxOMS branding or UI.
- Do not build a full enterprise resource scheduler yet.
- Do not build quoting/invoicing templates until Receipts & Invoices is ready.
- Keep Juniper Berry focused on live-event production planning and financial reconciliation.

## Recommended Roadmap Placement

After Access Control Alpha and Audit Trail Alpha:

1. Receipts & Invoices
2. My Auditor
3. Resource-aware calendar improvements
4. Venue maps/TicketLeap import
5. Exports and calendar feed

The resource-aware calendar should grow from Juniper Berry's existing event calendar, budget, revenue, settlement, and future receipt/invoice data. It should not become a generalized enterprise scheduling system until the live-event production workflow proves the need.
